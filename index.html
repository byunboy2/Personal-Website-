About Me

I originally majored in the life sciences, but have been working as
an accountant for the past several years. I worked with several
software engineers at my previous jobs to improve workflows and was
duly awed by the impact they made. From what I've observed, the
field involves a lot of creativity, collaboration and problem solving.
Plus it is constantly evolving so no day is ever the same and there
are limitless amount of opportunities. It amazes me how people from
all walks of life from different parts of the world could get together
build and scale applications that makes a positive impact and solve
challenging problems all at the comfort of their homes. Furthermore,
I truly believe software engineering is an egalitarian career. All one
really needs is a laptop, good internet and a drive to succeed in this field.
Inspired by the potential, I quit my job and enrolled in Rithm School
to become a software engineer.
